THE BRUJA'S EXPERIENCE

Your identity crafted with maximum precision and biosafety.

01. Vision & Design

We listen to your idea, answer any questions, and define the ideal size and placement. We create a custom design adapted exclusively to your body.

02. Preparing the Sanctuary

Sanitized Booth: Complete disinfection before your session. We prepare the space using only certified machinery and equipment approved for professional use.

03. Sterilization Protocol

Absolute Safety: All instruments undergo certified sterilization processes. We use 100% new, disposable, single-use needles, opened strictly in front of you.

04. The Session

We work with utmost precision and care, prioritizing your comfort. We use strict protective barriers (gloves, sleeves) complying with all current health regulations.

05. Aftercare

The ritual doesn't end when you leave the studio. We guide you step-by-step through the healing process to ensure a perfect and lasting result on your skin.
